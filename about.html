Purdue Undergraduate Reserach Expo

Jan 2021 - May 2021

I worked with a team to develop a waypoint-based approach towards autonomous driving. We did this by creating a convolutional neural network and simulations in Unity. We trained the NN in Unity by creating various driving simulations using various methods such as following a line of manually plotted points using PID. We then implemented the NN into Unity and were able to drive a car autonomously around a simulated Indianapolis Motor Speedway.
